Our Mission

Sharing the Word of God

Our institution's mission is to inspire youth as Diakonos, drawing from the ancient Greek roots of the word to serve as ministers of God's Word. We aim to equip our youth with the knowledge, skills, and passion to share the Gospel through acts of service and evangelism. Through our efforts, we hope to demonstrate the love and grace of Christ to those around us, and empower a new generation of believers to live out the call of Diakonos in their daily lives.

Empowering Youth

Our institution's mission is to empower youth leadership through the spirit of Diakonos, drawing from the ancient Greek roots of the word to inspire service and ministry. We believe that by equipping young leaders with the skills and knowledge needed to serve others, we can make a positive impact on our communities and the world. Through our commitment to leadership development, we hope to create a new generation of Diakonos, empowered to lead with compassion, humility, and a commitment to serving God and others.
