The Founder

Yohane Samuel Mollaneda Perez is a student at Philippine Science High School Southern Mindanao Campus. Despite challenges, Yohane has dedicated his life to serving God. He discovered his leadership abilities when elected class president and Grade 7 batch governor. These experiences ignited his passion to serve his community and led to the founding of Diakonos. The organization promotes service to God and fellow humans, inspiring others to use their leadership abilities for positive change.
